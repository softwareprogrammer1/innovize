Safety Systems

Innovize delivers robust, integrated Safety Systems for offshore, marine, and onshore environments where personnel safety and asset protection are paramount. Our expertise ensures seamless sub-system integration, rigorous testing, and compliance with global standards. We offer a comprehensive portfolio of Fire and Gas Detection, Emergency Shutdown, and Protection Systems, continually expanding to meet evolving client needs.

Fire & Gas Detection System (FGS)

Innovize Systems delivers integrated fire and gas detection solutions, identifying hazards and alerting personnel in compliance with IEC 61508 SIL2, NFPA 72, and EN54. Our centralized systems streamline monitoring, diagnostics, and maintenance, and scale to include ESD and HVAC/fire damper controls. We support a wide range of sensors for both fire and gas detection.

Addressable Fire Detection System (AFDS)

Our AFDS provides intelligent, addressable fire detection for critical areas, adhering to NFPA 72, EN54, IEC 61508, and SIL2. The system supports two-way communication, multi-criteria detectors, and seamless integration with third-party systems via Modbus.

High Sensitivity Smoke Detection System (HSSD)

Our aspirating HSSD solutions provide early fire detection by continuously sampling air, ideal for data centers, control rooms, and clean rooms. Addressable control panels and fire-resistant piping ensure reliability in demanding environments.

Emergency Shutdown System (ESD)

Our ESD systems ensure human safety and protect infrastructure and the environment when processes exceed safe limits. Certified with SIL ratings, these systems are highly available, fault-tolerant, and interface with F&G, fire damper, and PAGA systems.

Safety Instrumented System (SIS)

Our SIS solutions implement critical safety functions such as emergency and process shutdown, following IEC 61508 standards. We use leading PLC platforms and conduct comprehensive hazard analyses to ensure safe operation during hazardous situations.

Integrated Control & Safety System (ICSS)

Our ICSS platforms enable real-time monitoring and management of safety systems via HMI stations, ensuring high availability and operational reliability. Advanced diagnostics support effective fault tracing and preventive maintenance.

Fire Protection System

We deliver active fire protection systems tailored to customer needs, based on NFPA 72 Fire Safety Plans and certified by global and regional authorities. Our solutions include sprinkler, deluge, chemical, and gaseous suppression systems, all integrated with detection and control systems.

Fire Damper Control System

Our fire damper control solutions form part of passive fire protection, preventing fire propagation through HVAC ducts. The system actuates mechanical dampers and provides manual activation, with alarm signals integrated into central monitoring panels.

Fixed Gas Sampling System

We deliver fixed gas sampling systems for continuous monitoring in areas where fixed detectors are impractical. Our ATEX-certified solutions are ideal for hazardous environments and can be configured for single or multi-point sampling.
