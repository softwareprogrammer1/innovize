Who We Are

Innovize Pte. Ltd. is a specialized engineering & service provider of Electrical & Instrumentation (E&I) systems, serving Singapore and globally. Our expertise spans engineering design compliant with major class regulations (ABS, DNV, BV, etc.), system supply, testing, commissioning, and on-site after-sales support.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of integrated system solutions for the marine and offshore sectors — driving transformation through innovation, sustainability, and unmatched reliability. We aspire to empower clients to navigate tomorrow’s challenges with safety, efficiency, and environmental responsibility at the core of every operation.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering world-class system integration solutions tailored to the marine and offshore industries. Leveraging advanced technologies and deep sector knowledge, we develop solutions that optimize performance, enhance safety, and ensure sustainable operations across the globe.
