Control & Instrumentation Systems

At Innovize, we recognize that a reliable and expertly integrated Control & Instrumentation System is the backbone of any vessel, rig, or industrial facility. The control room serves as the nerve center, where a multitude of systems are monitored to ensure safety, efficiency, and optimal performance. Our solutions blend human expertise with advanced automation, delivering clarity, accuracy, and seamless operation for every client.

Advanced Fault Monitoring System (AFMS)

Our AFMS proactively monitors connected systems for abnormalities, alerting operators before failures occur. All alarms are logged in a built-in data logger, supporting maintenance teams with fault analysis and troubleshooting to minimize downtime.

Bulk Transfer Control Panel for Mud & Cement Tanks

Our bulk transfer control systems manage the safe transfer of mud and cement in offshore supply vessels and drilling rigs. Using PLCs and guided microwave level sensors, we automate valve control and tank level monitoring, with process visualization on HMI or mimic panels.

Cable Management & Accessories

We provide comprehensive cable management solutions and accessories, ensuring organized, safe, and efficient routing of electrical and instrumentation cabling throughout your facility.

Control and Alarm Monitoring Systems

We provide alarm monitoring solutions that notify operators of machinery status and critical alarms, supporting safe and efficient facility operation. Our user-friendly HMI displays alarms as text or graphics, with event logging and printing capabilities. Systems are tailored to operational needs and support both local and remote operator stations.

Control Cabinets & Consoles

Our custom-built control cabinets and operator consoles are designed for durability, safety, and ease of use. We ensure all assemblies meet international standards and are tailored to the specific requirements of each project, supporting seamless integration with all control and instrumentation subsystems.

Electrical / Instrumentation Test Panels

We supply and customize test panels for electrical equipment testing, equipped with certified instruments and built to IEC standards. Our panels include voltage transformers, circuit breakers, meters, and more, all arranged for efficient and safe operation in both onshore and offshore environments.

Emergency Shut Down Systems (ESD)

Our ESD systems are engineered to protect personnel, assets, and the environment by initiating safe shutdown procedures during critical events. Featuring redundant CPUs and robust communication, these systems are certified to international standards and integrate with fire & gas, damper, and alarm systems for comprehensive safety coverage.

Engine Alarm Monitoring System

Our engine alarm monitoring panels track critical engine parameters and provide clear HMI displays. When readings exceed safe thresholds, alarms are triggered with visual and audible alerts, supporting rapid operator response and equipment protection.

Hospital Call Alarm System

Our hospital call alarm systems enable immediate assistance requests in offshore and marine medical facilities. Patients can activate alarms via bedside buttons or pull-cords, with visual and audible alerts provided in medical and communal areas, ensuring rapid response.

HVAC Control System

We supply integrated HVAC control systems that interface with fire and gas detection, enabling automated fire damper activation and ventilation control. Our solutions use SIL-certified PLCs and touch screen HMIs, designed to meet international safety standards and customer specifications.

Industrial Automation Software Programming

Our team develops and implements industrial automation software, including PLC, DCS, and SCADA programming. We ensure seamless integration, user-friendly interfaces, and reliable process automation tailored to your operational requirements.

Liquid Level Measuring Systems

Our liquid level measurement systems provide accurate, real-time monitoring for a wide range of applications. We integrate field transmitters and sensors with PLC modules, converting signals into user-friendly graphical displays on HMI touch screens. Multiple alarm levels and communication protocols (HART, Modbus, Ethernet) ensure seamless integration with other monitoring systems.

Machinery Workshop Equipment

We supply custom-designed test benches and workshop equipment for electrical, electronic, pneumatic, and hydraulic testing. Each bench is equipped with calibrated, type-approved instruments and comes with comprehensive technical documentation and support.

Remote Valve Control System (RVCS)

Our RVCS solutions provide centralized control and monitoring of hydraulic, pneumatic, and electric motorized valves. Designed for compliance with major marine class regulations, these systems ensure reliable management of ballast and process valves, with custom HMI interfaces for operators.

Refrigerated Space Alarm

Our refrigerated space alarm systems are designed to alert personnel if someone is accidentally locked inside cold storage rooms. The system provides clear visual and audible signals, with location indication and integration options for broader facility monitoring.

Sensors & Load Cells

We offer a wide range of sensors and load cells for precise measurement and monitoring of process variables. Our solutions support integration with PLCs and HMIs, providing accurate data for process control and safety applications.

Tank Gauging Systems

We deliver advanced tank gauging solutions for monitoring the volume, mass, and level of various tanks, including ballast, water, fuel, and solids. Our systems utilize technologies such as radar, ultrasonic, electro-pneumatic transmitters, and both contact and contactless sensors, ensuring precise and reliable measurements for offshore and onshore facilities.

Vessel Monitoring System (VMS)

Our VMS is designed to monitor alarms and critical process conditions across marine and offshore facilities. Built as a scalable, distributed I/O system, it features redundant CPUs and servers, ensuring high reliability and minimal downtime. Remote I/O cabinets connect via redundant industrial protocols for robust communication.

Ventilation Failure Alarm System

Our ventilation failure alarm systems continuously monitor air pressure in critical areas such as cabins and control rooms. If pressure drops below safe levels, the system triggers alarms and can initiate emergency shutdowns, ensuring compliance with marine safety standards.

Weather Monitoring Systems

Our weather monitoring systems provide real-time data on environmental conditions, supporting safe and efficient operations in marine and industrial settings. Systems are customizable to meet specific site requirements.
