Telecom Systems

Innovize’s Telecom Systems deliver comprehensive, reliable solutions for the Oil & Gas industry and beyond. Our expertise in integrating Telecommunication, Safety, Automation, and Instrumentation Systems ensures seamless operations tailored to each client’s needs. We combine deep product knowledge with engineering excellence to provide robust, customized solutions for every environment.

PABX System

Innovize Systems supplies a wide range of PABX solutions, merging traditional and modern telephony for efficient internal and external communication. Our offerings include analogue, IP, hybrid PABX, IP telephony, and IP DECT systems, suitable for all environments and integrated with other facility systems.

Page / Party System

Our integrated page/party systems enable clear broadcast paging and simultaneous two-way conversations, with configurations for both safe and hazardous areas. These systems can be centrally or distributively amplified and are fully compatible with PABX and PAGA systems.

Public Address & Evacuation / General Alarm System

With decades of experience, Innovize delivers integrated PAGA systems certified for harsh and hazardous environments. Our microprocessor-based architecture ensures reliable communication, supervision, and alarm notification, with central monitoring and control.

Intercom / Talkback System

Our intercom and talkback systems provide reliable two-way and group voice communication, with modular networking and redundancy. VoIP, IP, and SIP technologies ensure flexibility, while integration with public address systems supports emergency announcements.

Local Area Network System

We configure LAN systems using advanced networking equipment to support secure, high-speed communication and resource sharing within facilities, using copper, fiber, or wireless links.

LAN Server System

Our centralized LAN server systems enable monitoring and storage of critical data, including process parameters, alarms, CCTV images, and more, with secure IP-based transmission and remote backup.

Telecommunication Network Monitoring System

Our TNMS solutions provide comprehensive monitoring, administration, and maintenance of complex hybrid networks, integrating fault, performance, configuration, and security monitoring in a scalable, real-time dashboard.

Wide Area Network System

Innovize offers WAN solutions using microwave, WiMAX, SDH, and fiber optics to connect geographically dispersed locations, supporting voice, data, and video traffic with high reliability and regulatory compliance.

Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) System

Our VSAT systems provide primary communication links between offshore and onshore facilities, supporting data, voice, video, and internet access across C-, Ku-, and Ka-bands, with integration to navigation and telephony systems.

Satellite TV Entertainment System

We supply Satellite TVRO/Entertainment systems for offshore and onshore facilities, offering both analogue and digital (IPTV) solutions with flexible distribution and integration options.

CCTV System

Our customized CCTV solutions enhance safety and asset protection, with analogue or IP-based systems that integrate with fire detection, intrusion detection, and access control for comprehensive monitoring.

Security System

We supply integrated or stand-alone security systems, including firewalls, access control, and gate control, tailored to protect assets from cyber and physical threats and to integrate with other facility systems.

Surveillance System

Our surveillance systems monitor activities and protect assets, providing alerts, event logs, and integration with other security and monitoring systems, including CCTV, GPS, and access control.

Access Control System

We deliver access control solutions using RFID, biometrics, electromagnetic locks, and barrier gates, all managed via IP-based software and integrated with other facility systems for comprehensive security.

Perimeter / Fence Intrusion Detection System

Our PIDS/FIDS solutions use advanced technologies—infrared, fiber optics, acoustic, shock, thermal, and radar—to monitor and protect facilities, with integration to CCTV and alarm systems for comprehensive security.

UHF Radio Communication System

We supply UHF radio systems for internal crew communication, with options for conventional and digital trunked systems, and integration with LAN, PABX, and PAGA for mission-critical requirements.

Microwave Radio / Wi-Max System

Our microwave radio and Wi-Max systems enable long-distance wireless communication for remote data collection, IP telephony, and video transmission, with support for frequency licensing and system configuration.

GMDSS System

We supply and commission Global Maritime Distress and Safety Systems (GMDSS) for vessels, ensuring compliance with IMO-SOLAS and maritime regulations for rapid emergency alerting and rescue coordination.

Aeronautical Radio Communication System

Our aeronautical radio systems ensure safe ground-to-air communication, compliant with ICAO regulations, and include NDB and VHF-AM components for navigation and weather updates.

Navigation System

We provide integrated navigation systems for vessels, including radar, GPS, AIS, ECDIS, autopilot, and more, all compliant with IMO regulations and tailored to vessel layouts.

Obstruction Light & Air-horn Systems

We deliver obstruction light and air-horn systems as part of navigation solutions, including marine lanterns, air/fog horns, and radar beacons, all compliant with marine safety regulations.

Helideck Lighting and Control System

Our helideck lighting and control systems provide visual landing aids for safe helicopter operations, compliant with ICAO, CAA, and IMO standards, and suitable for both safe and hazardous areas.

Weather / Helideck Monitoring System

We supply helideck monitoring systems for real-time meteorological and environmental data, supporting safe helicopter operations and integration with DPS, VMS, and loading computers.

Siren System

Our industrial siren systems provide high-intensity warning signals and mass notification, with options for pre-recorded or live voice messages and explosion-proof configurations for hazardous areas.

Clock Network System (Master Clock System)

We provide master clock systems for precise time synchronization across facilities, using GPS and NTP protocols, essential for operational tracking and event logging.

Sound Powered System

Our sound powered systems enable emergency communication between critical locations during shutdowns, operating independently of external power and supporting long-distance, multi-channel communication.
