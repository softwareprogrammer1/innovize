Fire Protection System

Innovize offers advanced Fire Protection Systems, engineered to deliver high-efficiency fire suppression while minimizing water usage and collateral damage. Our solutions are designed to meet the most stringent global standards, providing reliable protection for a wide range of applications, from industrial and marine to commercial and heritage environments.

Water-mist fire protection systems use ultra-fine water droplets to control, suppress, or extinguish fires. By producing a mist, these systems maximize surface area and cooling effect, rapidly reducing heat and oxygen at the fire source. This technology is highly effective for a variety of fire scenarios and is especially valued where water damage must be minimized.

Upon fire detection, the system activates and releases a fine spray of water mist. The tiny droplets absorb heat and quickly turn to steam, cooling the fire and displacing oxygen. This dual action blocks radiant heat and suppresses flames, while using significantly less water than traditional sprinkler systems.
